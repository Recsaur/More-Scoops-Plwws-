More Scoops Plws!!! is a game I created for Hackclub Milkyway! It's about serving customers scoops of ice cream, racking up points to buy upgrades, and getting highscores.

It's a very simple gameplay loop, but I made it pretty satisfying with effects and sounds! The project started I think late 2025, and shipped around late february, at currently a prototype state. It took around 40ish hours for coding, and 11hrs for making the art, so a total of around 51 hours on the project. I definitely have more planned for it, more content such as more customers, vips, shop items, abilities, gamemodes, and a WHOLE lot more that I had to hold myself from adding because of scope creep (and the project is suppose to be a prototype anyway). Spent a ton of effort on this, got great feedback from friends and people on the slack! Really motivates me a lot to keep continuing on this project and coding more stuff in the future^^
